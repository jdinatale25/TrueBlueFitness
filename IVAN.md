## About Ivan

Ivan is a personal trainer and coach currently pursuing a Master's degree in Psychology. He is bilingual, coaching in both English and Spanish.

Ivan's goal as a personal trainer is to educate his clients and bring them to confidence and independence. He wants the people he works with to make sustainable choices — not quick fixes. He sees himself as a coach, not just a trainer, and his greatest passion is helping people build long-lasting habits.

- **Coach-first mindset** — focused on the whole person, not just the workout
- **Habit-driven** — building long-lasting habits is his top priority
- **Sustainability over shortcuts** — encourages sustainable lifestyle choices
