Ivan's Story

Education, Habits,
and Real Results.

Ivan is a Certified Personal Trainer (CPT) who got into fitness because he believes movement, knowledge, and mindset are three of the most practical tools a person can have. He wanted to teach that.

On top of his certification, Ivan is pursuing a Master's in Psychology. That combination shapes how he coaches. Changing your body requires changing your habits and your thinking, and he takes that seriously. An app can give you a program. Ivan tries to give you an understanding.

Ivan coaches in English and Spanish. For his Spanish-speaking clients, that means working with someone who thinks and communicates in the same language they do, without workarounds.

He works with clients in Fort Worth in person and with clients anywhere through remote coaching. The remote option exists because consistency is the hardest part of fitness, and a lot of people need something that holds up when life gets unpredictable. Travel, odd schedules, moving to a new city: remote coaching keeps the work going regardless.

His goal with every client is the same: educate them, build their confidence, and make them less dependent on a coach over time. He considers it a win when a client no longer needs him.

What Ivan Believes

The Values Behind
True Blue Fitness

These are the principles behind every program and every client relationship.

📚

Education First

Ivan explains what he's doing and why. Every program comes with the reasoning behind it, so clients can apply that thinking beyond the gym and stop second-guessing themselves.

💪

Confidence Over Comparison

Ivan coaches clients toward their own goals, not some standard version of what fitness is supposed to look like. Progress is measured against where you started, not against anyone else.

🌱

Sustainable by Design

No crash diets, no extreme programs. Ivan builds habits that hold up when life gets busy, because a plan that falls apart under real conditions isn't much of a plan.

💻

In-Person or Remote

Ivan trains clients in Fort Worth and remotely with clients anywhere. Remote coaching fits people who travel, have unpredictable schedules, or live outside the area. The programming and accountability are the same either way.

🧠

Mind & Body Together

As a CPT pursuing a Master's in Psychology, Ivan approaches habit change, motivation, and the mental side of fitness with a depth most trainers don't have. Most programs skip this entirely. Ivan doesn't.

🗝️

Independence as the Goal

Ivan's job is to make himself unnecessary. The coaching ends; the habits don't. That's the whole point.

The Mission

Building confidence and independence,
one habit at a time.

True Blue Fitness exists to give every client the tools and knowledge to live an active, healthy life on their own terms, regardless of where they're starting or what language they speak.
